How It Works

Geckonnect re-images the recruiting timeline, guiding candidates through every step.

Are you having trouble finding the perfect career fit for your experience? Geckonnect aims to help applicants by providing suggestions for the best job fits on the market, based on your resume. It's simple, you upload your resume, we do some fancy matching through our recommender system, and you get a list of the best options to apply to. Once you apply, we help you along the way by visualizing each step of the recruitment process, notifying you of changes, and giving your detailed information.
